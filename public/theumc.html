The United Methodist Church

The mission of the United Methodist Church is to make disciples of Jesus Christ for the transformation of the world.

The United Methodist Church is an international demonination with members all over the world. The UMC has many online resources available for those seeking to learn more about our church, our beliefs, our people, or our ministries.
